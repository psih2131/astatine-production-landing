Events Reimagined Through Light and Performance

At Astatine Production, we specialize in elevating live events through powerful creative direction, immersive lighting concepts, and visionary performances.

We don't just design shows — we craft atmospheres, choreograph energy, and shape emotional journeys through light, movement, and concept. Each project begins with an idea — an atmosphere, a feeling — and unfolds into an unforgettable experience where light, motion, and intention merge into a living narrative.

Our expertise seamlessly unites:

  • Narrative-driven event concept development
  • Tailored lighting worlds and timecode programming excellence
  • Signature performance acts with avant-garde aesthetics
  • Immersive 3D visual previews that bring ideas to life

Whether we're transforming a stage, enhancing a gala, or building a one-of-a-kind act, we deliver bold, intelligent, and emotionally charged environments that redefine what an event can be.

We shape moments that become memory.

Our Handpicked Featured Services

We design the system, direct the ecosystem, and run the show — with one accountable ASTATINE standard.

01

Creative Direction & Show Creation

We build the show concept: tone, pacing, emotional arc, and stage language. A clear creative vision that translates into a clean plan.

02

Artistic Programming & Talent Curation

We curate artists and specialty acts that fit the brand and audience. Casting, contracting, rehearsals, and integration into the run-of-show.

03

Choreography & Performance Production

Custom choreography, movement direction, and act development. From idea to stage-ready performance.

04

Experience Design & Decor Concept

Visual themes, decor direction, florals/catering coordination (as needed), and room flow. Designed to feel intentional—never generic.

05

Executive Production Leadership

We lead the full operation: departments, schedule, decisions, and on-site authority. One plan. No vendor gaps.

06

Stage Concept & System Design

Layout, sightlines, camera angles, technical plan, and build logic. Designed for broadcast-ready results.

07

Visual System Architecture (LED + Video)

Processing, routing, playback discipline, and content readiness. Engineered for camera-clean results.

08

Lighting Architecture & Programming

Lighting environment that supports the story and the camera. Partners supply when needed — we control the outcome.

09

Audio & Rigging Coordination

Integrated planning across power, rigging, safety, and logistics. Clean handoffs under one technical direction.

10

Rehearsals, Cueing & Showtime Control

Run-of-show structure, cueing, and real-time control on site. No surprises at showtime.

11

Risk & Redundancy Planning

Backups, spares, and decision protocols built into the plan. Built for high-stakes corporate timelines.
